Our Story

Rooted in craft,
grown with care

Oak & Bean was founded on one belief: a great cup of coffee should do more than taste good. It should slow you down. Since opening our doors on High Street in 2018, we've sourced our beans from small family farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.

Every drink is crafted by hand, every pastry is baked fresh each morning, and the music is always just low enough to let you think.
